Accessibility Matters

Web accessibility ensures that people of all abilities can interact with your content. By using semantic tags, alt text for images, and ARIA labels where needed, your site becomes more inclusive.

SEO Basics

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) improves how your content appears in search results. Use proper headings, descriptive meta tags, and structured content to boost discoverability.
